The context menu is keyboard accessible. 

Press Shift+F10 or the Applications key to open the context menu on a tree item.
Press the Up and Down Arrow keys to move focus between the menu options.
Press the Left and Right Arrow keys to open or close submenus if applicable.
Press any letter or number to jump to a menu item starting with that character or digit.
Press Enter to activate a menu option.
Press Escape to close the currently open menu.
Press Tab to close the menu and all submenus.
Mouse users can right click on a tree item to open the menu as usual.
Mouse users can cancel and close the menu by clicking anywhere else on the page.
On a touch screen, touch a tree item and hold for about 2.5 seconds then release to open the menu,
tap a menu item to activate it, or tap anywhere else on the page to cancel and close the menu.

The ARIA Tree control is keyboard accessible. 

Set focus to the ARIA Tree.
Press the Up and Down Arrow keys to navigate vertically.
Press the Left and Right Arrow keys to collapse or expand branch nodes.
Press any letter or number to jump to a tree node starting with that character or digit.
Press Enter or the Spacebar to activate a node.
Mouse users can click tree branches and nodes to expand/collapse or activate items as usual.
